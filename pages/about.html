About Me



My name is Ben Winters. I am a Software Engineer graduating from Brigham Young University with a degree in Computer Science.

My emphasis has been in Software Testing, Analysis and Verification. I worked in Dr. Eric Mercer's lab performing verification on open source cryptographic algorithms against their NIST specifications.

In my spare time, I love to be in the outdoors climbing and hiking with my wife and daughter.
